## Summary of course/Syllabus
To understand the digital logic fundamentals and necessary concepts of Boolean algebra and develop the ability to design combinational and sequential logic circuits with required analysis.

## Reference text
1. Thomas L. Floyd and R.P. Jain “Digital Fundamentals” Parson Education, 8th Edition.
2. Charles H Roth Jr. “Fundamentals of Logic Design”, Cengage Learning, 5th Edition, India Edition.
3. M Morris Mano and Michael D. Ciletti, “Digital Design”, PHI, 4th Edition, Eastern Economy Edition.
4. Malvino & Leach “Digital Principles and Applications”, 7th Edition, McGraw-Hill Education.

## No of instructional hours
52

## Course outcomes
1. Understand digital logic concepts like logic gates, logic families, flip flops and memory devices.
2. Acquire knowledge of various number systems and binary codes and develop ability for code conversion as per requirement.
3. Apply concepts of Boolean algebra and logic simplification techniques to design digital circuits.
4. Design of combinational logic and sequential logic circuits.
5. Analysis of various digital logic circuits using simulation tool.

## Pre reqs
Elementary knowledge of science and mathematics
